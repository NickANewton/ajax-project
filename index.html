  • Hunter x Hunter

    Rating

    Long, but REALLY good!

    What a wild ride! While long, this anime had me glued to my seat gripping with anticipation throughout. The adventrues of Gon and the crew were creative, engaging, and just plain fun! Each character was unique, and trust me, there are quite a few of them! The Chimera Ant arc did drag on, but for a good reason. You really got a sense of each characters identity (on both sides). When a story has you feeling for the “enemy” you just know you have a brilliant story teller behind the scenes. I thoroughly enjoyed this anime and would 100% watch it again! 5 out of 5 stars!
